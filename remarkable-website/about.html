About Us

At our core, we believe in the power of exhibitions to inspire, educate, and entertain. We strive to create unique experiences that not only showcase the latest products and services but also foster collaboration, networking, and growth opportunities for all attendees.

About Us

project Video

Remarkable FZE is a well-respected company with more than thirty years of experience. We offer the complete exhibition and shop fitting services. We have a proven track record of success. We design and manufacture innovative, high quality temporary and permanent exhibition stands and structures for national and international clients including Saudi Arabia, UK, Dutch, Finnish and French government groups, Shell, BP, Equinor, Petrofac, Jaguar & Land Rover, Perkins, Total, Canon, Beats, Virgin, GDF Suez and many more.

Remarkable FZE is ideally located with offices and production centers in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, UK, Brazil and Russia for exhibitions and conferences across the globe. This allows us to provide full turn-key services for any demand that requires the client (furniture hire, logistics etc). As we take care of the design and production "in house", working with our own teams of craftsmen, we are able to keep the quality high, the costs down and give a quick turnaround on each individual project. Our finish product can be transported with our own vehicles or by container to any worldwide destination.
